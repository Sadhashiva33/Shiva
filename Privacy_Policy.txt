Privacy Policy – Kaida Discord Bot
Effective Date: 10 August 2025

1. Information We Collect
Kaido may collect:

Discord User IDs, usernames, and nicknames (to identify users for commands, moderation logs, and TTS).

Server IDs and channel IDs (to operate commands and send messages).

Voice channel activity (to detect joins/leaves for TTS).

Command usage logs (for debugging and performance tracking).

2. How We Use Your Information
To provide bot features (music playback, TTS, moderation, etc.).

To improve bot performance and fix bugs.

To enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties, except as required by law or to comply with Discord’s policies.

4. Data Retention
Minimal logs may be stored for up to 30 days for debugging and security.

5. Your Rights
You may request deletion of your data by contacting the bot owner via Discord.

6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Kaido after updates means you agree to the changes.
